RECIPE OF THE DAY: BRAISED MUSHROOMS

Need inspiration? Take a look at our recipe of the day below, for a quick and easy meat-free meal!

Ingredients

  • Splash of olive oil
  • 6 medium flat mushrooms
  • 6 spring onions, chopped, plus extra to serve
  • A thumb-sized piece ginger, chopped
  • 1 red chilli, finely chopped
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • A pinch of caster sugar
  • 150g basmati rice
  • 2 tsp toasted sesame oil

Method

To start off...


Heat the oil in a large frying pan. Fry the mushrooms for 3-4 minutes, turning as they cook. Add 1/2 the spring onions and all the ginger, chilli, soy sauce and sugar. Keep turning the mushrooms in the sauce until they are cooked and the sauce is glossy but still liquid.

...Final Steps


Cook the basmati following pack instructions then stir through the rest of the spring onions and the sesame oil. Spoon the rice into bowls, add the mushrooms and spoon over the sauce. Finish with a few more chopped spring onions, if you like.
